# Output Template

Use this exact structure for the daily blog post output.

## Frontmatter

```yaml
---
title: "Daily Tech & GNSS News Digest - [Month Day, Year]"
description: "Today's top business-impacting stories from tech and GNSS industries: [brief 1-line summary of top stories]"
date: YYYY-MM-DD
category: "News Digest"
tags: ["Tech News", "GNSS", "Daily Digest", ...additional relevant tags]
hero: "/images/daily-digest.jpg"
draft: false
---
```

## Body Structure

```markdown
[Opening paragraph: 2-3 sentences summarizing the day's most significant developments across both tech and GNSS sectors. Set the context for why these stories matter.]

## Tech News

[Brief intro sentence about today's tech landscape if needed]

### [Story 1 Headline - Most Important]

[2-3 paragraphs covering the story with business analysis. What happened, why it matters, who's affected.]

### [Story 2 Headline]

[2-3 paragraphs]

### [Story 3 Headline]

[1-2 paragraphs for less major stories]

---

## GNSS News

[Brief intro sentence about GNSS industry developments if needed]

### [Story 1 Headline - Most Important]

[2-3 paragraphs covering the story with industry analysis. Focus on implications for precision positioning, autonomous systems, agriculture, surveying, etc.]

### [Story 2 Headline]

[2-3 paragraphs]

### [Story 3 Headline]

[1-2 paragraphs for less major stories]

---

## Key Takeaways

- [Bullet point 1: Most actionable insight from tech news]
- [Bullet point 2: Most actionable insight from GNSS news]
- [Bullet point 3: Any cross-sector implications or trends]
```

## Tag Guidelines

Always include base tags: `"Tech News"`, `"GNSS"`, `"Daily Digest"`

Add topic-specific tags based on content:
- Tech: `"AI"`, `"Semiconductors"`, `"Big Tech"`, `"Startups"`, `"Regulation"`
- GNSS: `"RTK"`, `"PPP"`, `"Autonomous Vehicles"`, `"Agriculture"`, `"Surveying"`, `"Multi-constellation"`

## Filename Convention

`YYYY-MM-DD-daily-tech-gnss-news.mdx`

Example: `2026-01-12-daily-tech-gnss-news.mdx`
